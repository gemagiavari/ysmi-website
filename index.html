OUR SERVICES

Founded by dr. Syafari Soma, Sp.KJ & dr. Rama Giovani, Sp.KJ, the Indonesia Mental Health Foundation has provided comprehensive and high quality outpatient medical services to the people of Bandung and beyond since 2018. We believe in maintaining the health of our community, and we are committed to exceeding the expectations of our patients by leading service. Let us give you the best care you deserve.
